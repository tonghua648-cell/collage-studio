MANUAL

Input

Drop an image onto the canvas or use Select Image. Remove clears the source and resets the preview.

Levels

Black, Gamma, and White reshape the grayscale source before posterization.

Posterization

Levels controls how many tone bands the image is split into for collage mapping.

Gradient Map

Each tone has a color swatch and a material preview. Upload a texture to replace a flat color.

Material Edit

Click a material preview, then drag on the canvas to move it. Wheel scales it. Shift plus wheel rotates it.

Export & Advice

Export saves the current collage as PNG. Advice opens a rating and feedback panel.
